Thoughts

  • On web design

    Responsive Design!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Responsive Design!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
    Note: I'm new to web design.

    I think, and this my personal stance, that for web design to be a fruitful project, HTML is obviously used for structure.
    CSS is the crown of it all - it is tough to not fall for CSS. Bootstrap, and Javascript I believe should be used intentionally and only when necessary. The necessity of their use is obviously subjective, but I do think a clear temptation to over-use or abuse these tools exists here. Did someone say Javascript frameworks?


  • On Film

    Is the film camera representing the viewer's POV or is it a suggestion for a viewing mental model?
    Why do I ask this?

    Watching film is only possible because of filming. This is then broadcast to us, the viewers. One picture at a time. From multiple perspectives.
    That one fact alone is probably a giveaway, but I wasn't aware that the camera wasn't meant to be in stead of my view. I guess it is meant to be a "state of mind", whose 'state of mind' ? It seems absurd to me that I should occupy these many positions as a film camera suggests. One instance I'm closer, the next? seeing from the side. Film is exciting for it though, I believe. But I also believe this is when Theatre gets its edge and suggestion.
